Our Services

At CompNet, we offer a comprehensive range of repair, upgrade, and support services for your computers and peripherals. Our experienced technicians are here to help you get the most out of your technology.

Hardware Repair Services

PC & Laptop Repair

Is your computer running slow, not turning on, or making strange noises? We diagnose and repair all types of issues for desktops and laptops of all brands. This includes motherboard repair/replacement, screen replacement, power supply issues, overheating problems, and more.

Common Repairs: Broken screens, failing hard drives, faulty RAM, keyboard/trackpad issues, charging port problems.

Price: Starting from £45 (Diagnostics fee may apply, redeemable against repair cost)

Peripheral Repair

Don't let a faulty peripheral slow you down. We repair a variety of computer accessories.

Includes: Keyboard repair (key replacement, cleaning), mouse repair (button issues, sensor problems), printer troubleshooting and basic repairs, monitor diagnostics (e.g., dead pixels, connection issues).

Price: Starting from £25

Upgrades & Installations

Performance Upgrades

Give your computer a speed boost! We offer component upgrades to enhance performance and extend the life of your machine.

Popular Upgrades: SSD (Solid State Drive) installation for significantly faster boot times and application loading, RAM (Memory) upgrades for better multitasking, Graphics Card upgrades for improved gaming and creative work.

Price: Starting from £50 (plus cost of components)

Software Installation & Setup

We can help you install and configure operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux), productivity software, antivirus programs, and other applications. We also offer new PC/laptop setup services.

Price: Starting from £30

Software & Data Services

Virus & Malware Removal

Protect your valuable data and privacy. Our technicians are skilled in removing viruses, spyware, malware, and other malicious software to restore your computer's security and performance.

Price: Starting from £40

Data Recovery & Backup Solutions

Lost important files? We offer data recovery services for failing hard drives and accidental deletion (success not guaranteed). We can also help you set up robust data backup solutions to prevent future data loss.

Price: Data recovery from £70 (evaluation fee may apply), Backup setup from £35

Our Pricing Approach

We believe in transparent and fair pricing. For many common services, we offer fixed rates. For more complex issues or diagnostics, an initial assessment fee may apply, which is often waived if you proceed with the repair. We will always provide you with a detailed quote before any work begins.

All repairs come with a 90-day warranty on parts and labour.
